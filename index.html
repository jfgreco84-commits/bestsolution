Good to Know

What is C5?
C5 is a fine metal restoration lotion. It brings back the shine on metals that polish alone can't reach. It is not a cloth or a wipe, it's a lotion you work in. Pairs perfectly with your Best Solution polish.
What can Best Solution restore?
Jewelry (rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, watches), eyewear, watches, coins & collectibles, home & kitchen (faucets, sinks, silverware, serving trays), and auto & hobby (chrome, motorcycle parts, tools). No acid, no ammonia, no abrasives.
